Biography

Born in France in 1986, Annick Vavasseur is a contemporary painter whose practice explores the relationships between memory, perception, and inner landscapes.

After studying English literature, art history, and traditional decorative painting techniques—including wood graining, faux marble, decorative finishes, and panoramic mural recreation—she completed a Master's degree in Visual Arts Research at Panthéon-Sorbonne University.

Her practice brings together traditional craftsmanship and academic research, and has been further enriched through workshops in painting, watercolor, life drawing, drawing, and perspective at Paris Ateliers, the Ateliers des Beaux-Arts de la Ville de Paris, and the Louvre Museum.
